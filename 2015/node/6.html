iKOS

About us
We are Drupal development professionals, specialising in eCommerce.

iKOS is part of the Inviqa Group, which consists of four companies; Inviqa, Session Digital, SensioLabs UK, and iKOS. As a group, we have offices in London, Edinburgh, Brighton, Sheffield, Liverpool, Manchester, Leeds, Glasgow, Munich, Kaunas (Lithuania), Hódmezővásárhely (Hungary), Bratislava and Nove Zamky (Slovakia).
 
iKOS are hiring 
As a Drupal Developer at iKOS, you’ll have the chance to join some of the most exciting, challenging Drupal based projects available. Our projects tend to be for blue chip companies, and you’ll be working with the best Drupal developers in the industry, on a variety of complex projects.

It goes without saying that you know your Drupal – really, really well. 
 
About You
  • You’ll have a thorough working knowledge of Drupal development best practices
  • You’re a self-starting coder and well versed in various technologies/languages
  • You’ll have significant experience of Drupal development in a commercial environment
  • You’ll aspire to become a high-level Drupal consultant and/or a developer, and have a drive to be challenged and expand your skills
  • You’ll have strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • You’ll be a team player, and have good communication skills
  • You’re actively seeking an exciting new opportunity
  • You’ll have solid experience implementing Drupal applications, as well as a keen interest in software development best practices
  • You’ll be able to regularly commute to one of our offices
  • You’ll need to be eligible to work in the UK
  • Having a good computer science related degree, or equivalent, would be an advantage

What We Offer
You have an amazing opportunity to join an exciting and dynamic company, working with some of the leading lights of the open source industry. Our employees value the flexible working environment, as well as the opportunity to develop their skills on a wide variety of interesting projects.

We offer a competitive rates and benefits. Our technical staff receive regular training and are encouraged to attend Drupal/open source conferences 

Interested? 
If you are interested in this position at iKOS, please send your CV, your salary expectations, and your current employment status (with details of necessary notice period, if relevant) to careers@i-kos.com.
