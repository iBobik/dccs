5. - 7.6.2015, Bratislava

DrupalCampCS
Three days of Drupal action!

Training: Start with Drupal now!

IS THIS RIGHT FOR YOU?
I have been heard from many of my previous trainees they attended Drupal Camps with the specific goal of learning to use Drupal. Learning to use Drupal as a complete beginner seems to be be what they have been after.

If this is why you are coming along to Drupal Camp CS then this training is for you.

This one day taught Drupal 7 course is designed for the absolute beginner who wants to get going with Drupal as quickly as possible.

WHAT EXPERIENCE DO I NEED?
You do not need to have previous experience of any content management framework, nor do you need to be in very technical in terms of HTML, JavaScript, and any PHP. There will be NO CODING. Everything is click and build.

WHAT TO BRING
Please bring along a CAPABLE wireless-enabled Mac or a PC over which you have FULL ADMINISTRATIVE RIGHTS.
By 'CAPABLE' I mean a decent speed (i5 / i7) machine with enough RAM as to be workable and preferably an SSD.
By 'FULL' I mean full - you’ll need to be able to install software and you’ll need to access command console.

CONTENT

  1. Installing Drupal locally
  2. Creating simple content
  3. Structure: Regions & blocks
  4. Site configuration
  5. User management
  6. Experimenting with modules
  7. Roles and permissions
  8. Building content types
  9. Categorising content
  10. Creating views of content
  11. URL alias patterns
  12. Managing menus
  13. Optimising performance
  14. A brief look at Drupal SEO
  15. Drupal distributions (accelerators)
  16. Applying Drupal themes
  17. What next?

TAKEWAYS
A Drupal-optimised local stack installed on your local laptop.
A basic site hand-built from scratch on the day which demonstrates everything covered in the course.
Slides from the day

See you all then for a busy day.
