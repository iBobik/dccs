Venue

Where?

Event will be held in Faculty of Informatics and Information Technologies. Faculty of Informatics and Information Technologies (FIIT) is one of the seven faculties of the Slovak University of Technology in Bratislava (SUT). FIIT SUT covers the very area of informatics and information technologies (IIT) in both research and education. FIIT is the first faculty in the Slovak Republic with such mission. In spite of the fact, that FIIT is a new faculty, SUT has long-term experiences in the area of IIT and has achieved several substantial results in this area. A base for the FIIT formation was the place of work, which guaranteed education and research in IIT at the Slovak University of Technology till origination of the Faculty of Informatics and Information Technologies. Faculty offers one of the newest and best venue for this-sized events in Bratislava. It is located in locality called Mlynska dolina. This locality hosts multiple high schools and even campus.

How to get there?

From railway station Bratislava hlavna stanica:

  • take bus no. 32 direction "Dlhe Diely" and get off on stop "ZOO"
  • take bus no. 93 direction "Vysehradska", change at stop "Zochova" to line 31/39 direction Cintorin Slavicie udolie and get off on stop "ZOO"

From railway station Bratislava Petrzalka:

  • take bus no. 80 direction "Kolarovo namestie", get off on stop "Zochova", use subway to get across the road and take line 31/39 direction "Cintorin Slavicie udolie" and get off on stop "ZOO"
  • take bus no. 93 direction "Hlavna stanica", get off on stop "Zochova", use subway to get across the road and take line 31/39 direction "Cintorin Slavicie udolie" and get off on stop "ZOO"
  • take bus no. 91 direction "Novy most", get off on stop "Novy most", take bus no. 37 direction "Záhorská Bystrica" and get off on stop "ZOO"
  • take bus no. 91 direction "Novy most", get off on stop "Novy most", take tram 4/9 direction "Dubravka"/"Karlova Ves" and get off on stop "Botanicka zahrada"

From bus station:

  • take bus no. 21 direction "Devinska Nova Ves", get off on stop "Patronka", use subway to get across the road and take line 30/37 direction "Novy most" and get off on stop "ZOO"

From airport:

  • take bus no. 61 direction "Hlavna stanica", get off on stop "Hlavna stanica" and follow steps directions in section "From railway station Bratislava hlavna stanica"

You need to buy ticket for public transportation in advance from ticket machines. Basic ticket for 15minutes ride/no changes costs 0.70€. Ticket for 1h ride/with changes costs 0.90€. Tickets must be validated right after you enter vehicle. You need additional ticket for your luggage.
