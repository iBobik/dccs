About tickets

This year we prepared 2 day conference for you - 1 sessions day and 1 day of trainings. During sessions day there will be 2 track - Czechoslovakian and English.

Last year you were satisfied with coffee and catering options. This year we want to stick with the same coffee provider. For catering, we are looking for possibility of having warm dishes for lunch and some light refreshment during all day.

For trainings, entrance fee includes warm lunch and drinks, coffee and tea through the day.

What is included in your tickets?
  • access to sessions day
  • food and drinks (non-alcoholic, coffee/tea) through sessions day
What is not included in your tickets?
  • access to trainings
  • T-shirt
  • Drinks and food on social event

Why are tickets more expensive than last year?
This year we are selling tickets through different ticketing platform, which is charging us amonth, you can see in Fee column in table below, per each ticket. Except this Fee, they are also charging us VAT as we aren't VAT payers, which is another 20% from ticket price.

If you have problem to pay requested entrance fee, contact us on info@drupal.sk and we will figure something out.
